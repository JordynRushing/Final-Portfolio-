Learn About Me

Bringing Ideas to Life Through Bold, Minimalist Design: Jordyn T. Rushing, a graphic designer and digital artist dedicated to crafting thoughtful, impactful visuals. Explore my portfolio to see how I blend creativity and strategy to create designs that inspire and connect.

Hi, I'm Jordyn T. Rushing — a passionate graphic designer, digital artist, and creative problem solver. I specialize in crafting modern, intuitive, and visually captivating designs that merge functionality with aesthetic appeal. My work embodies a dynamic blend of Minimalism, Typography-Centric Design, Pop Art, and Illustrative Elements, reflecting my diverse artistic influences and versatile skill set.


With experience in freelance and collaborations, such as my work with Alpha Resources, I've honed my expertise in cover art, ads, and logo graphics, delivering impactful visuals that leave a lasting impression. As a graphic design student at LSU, I'm continuously expanding my knowledge, pushing creative boundaries, and integrating innovative design solutions into my projects.


Beyond design, I'm deeply passionate about community engagement and uplifting other Black artists. I've volunteered for initiatives like LSU's Geaux Big event and love using my creative skills to make a positive impact.


When I'm not designing, you'll find me drawing, creating digital art, swimming, or capturing moments through photography. My portfolio is a reflection of my dedication to creativity, storytelling, and helping others bring their visions to life.
